A paper titled ["Communication-efficient ADMM using Gaussian process regression and fully adaptive uniform quantization"](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S2773186326001039) by Aldo Duarte, Binh Nguyen, Truong X. Nghiem, and Shuangqing Wei has been published in [Franklin Open](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S2773186326001039). This paper presents a communication-efficient distributed optimization framework for ADMM with adaptive quantization strategies that adjust mid-value, window length, and resolution at each iteration. It also introduces uncertainty-aware communication and bit-allocation mechanisms and a hybrid switching approach to maintain convergence near stagnation. Simulations on a distributed quadratic cost-sharing problem show a strong trade-off between communication reduction and solution accuracy, with the hybrid method providing the most robust and consistent performance.
